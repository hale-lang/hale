# office/

The Quiet Office. Spells × technology × multiverse.

**What goes here.** The philosophical layer of Aperio, written
in the register of the Office — reconstructed annexes,
concordance notes, marginalia, the occasional ferryman's field
report. Not docs in the conventional sense; fiction-fused-with-
essay. The big claim ("Aperio names the shape every senior
engineer reconstructs") lives here, in the voice that lets it
be claimed.

**Reader.** The curious, the depth-seeker, the person pulled in
by the larger frame rather than the language or the tool. Also:
anyone who needs the pitch but wants to read it from the inside
of the world rather than the outside.

**Source seeds.** Two roots feed this track:

- `../archive/fiction/annex-l-7704.md` — the original Office
  sketch (the Brace Tower, Iren of Adde, Mevr's marginalia,
  the secondary editor). The register was first found here.
- `../archive/docs/src/grimoire/` — the meta-spell framing
  (Aperio as the spell of spellcasting, the wand, the four
  moments). The other root.

The office register and the grimoire register were drafted
in parallel and never quite met; this track is where they
fuse.

**Status.** Empty scaffold. Content arrives in purpose-driven
sessions.
